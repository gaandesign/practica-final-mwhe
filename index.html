ABOUT US

Häagen-Dazs™ have been making ice cream like no other since 1960. Our founder, Reuben Mattus’ vision was to make the most extraordinary ice cream the world has ever tasted, a spoonful of luxury to escape the everyday. And to get there he only used the highest quality, carefully selected ingredients. As Reuben said, ‘If you’re like everyone else, you’re lost’. So we make an ice cream like no other – the best that best can be.

Häagen-Dazs was founded in 1961 by Reuben and Rose Mattus in the Bronx, New York. Their vision was to create a premium ice cream that would stand out from the mass-produced varieties available at the time. Inspired by European ice cream traditions, they combined only the finest ingredients to create a rich, indulgent treat. Over the years, Häagen-Dazs has become a symbol of excellence, offering a wide range of flavors that continue to delight customers worldwide. Today, the brand is recognized for its commitment to quality and innovation in the ice cream industry.

At Häagen-Dazs, our mission is simple: to create the finest ice cream in the world using the highest-quality ingredients. We believe that ice cream should be an indulgent, extraordinary experience, and we’re dedicated to pushing the boundaries of flavor, texture, and creativity. With a passion for excellence, we continually strive to delight our customers by offering unique, unforgettable flavors that inspire joy and bring people together.

Häagen-Dazs is known for its luxurious and creamy ice cream made from the finest ingredients. Some of our most beloved signature products include:
Vanilla:
A classic, rich flavor made with premium vanilla beans.

Chocolate Belgian:
A decadent chocolate ice cream made with Belgian chocolate.

Salted Caramel:
A sweet and savory indulgence combining rich caramel with a touch of sea salt.

Cookies & Cream:
A favorite, featuring smooth vanilla ice cream with crunchy chocolate cookie pieces.

Dulce de Leche:
A creamy, caramel-infused treat with a touch of sweetness. These flavors, along with many others, have made Häagen-Dazs a beloved brand worldwide.

Häagen-Dazs is available in over 80 countries, with thousands of locations around the world. From the United States to Europe, Asia, and beyond, our premium ice cream is enjoyed by millions. Whether in local supermarkets, boutique shops, or dedicated Häagen-Dazs stores, we are committed to bringing our high-quality ice cream to ice cream lovers globally. As we continue to expand, Häagen-Dazs remains a symbol of luxury, craftsmanship, and innovation, providing customers with an exceptional dessert experience wherever they are.

VALUES

QUALITY

Quality is not just a word—it’s the heart of everything we do. From the moment we source our ingredients to the final scoop in your bowl, we uphold the highest standards to ensure every bite is a moment of indulgence. We use only the finest, all-natural ingredients, including pure cream, fresh milk, and carefully selected flavors like Madagascar vanilla and Belgian chocolate. Each step in our production process is meticulously controlled to preserve the richness, texture, and taste that have defined our brand for decades. When you choose Häagen-Dazs, you’re choosing a product crafted with care and dedication, because we believe that exceptional quality is the foundation of extraordinary taste.

SUSTAINABILITY

At Häagen-Dazs, we recognize our responsibility to care for the planet we all share. That’s why sustainablity is at the core of our operations. From sourcing ingredients responsibly to adopting eco-friendly packaging solutions, we are committed to minimizing our environmental impact. Our dairy suppliers adhere to sustainable farming practices that prioritize animal welfare and reduce carbon footprints. Additionally, we work to ensure that every step of our supply chain contributes to a healthier planet, including our efforts to reduce water and energy consumption. Sustainablity isn’t just about preserving resources—it’s about leaving a legacy of environmental stewardship for generations to come. Together, we can savor our favorite flavors while protecting the world around us.

CREATIVITY

Häagen-Dazs isn’t just about ice cream—it’s about pushing the boundaries of what’s possible in flavor and design. Creativity is at the heart of every product we develop, from innovative flavors like Salted Caramel and Matcha Green Tea to limited-edition collaborations that surprise and delight our customers. We believe that ice cream is a canvas for artistic expression, and we strive to bring that artistry to life in every pint and scoop. Beyond flavors, we’re constantly reimagining how we connect with our customers, from unique packaging designs to inspiring advertising campaigns. Our goal is to inspire joy and spark imagination, ensuring that every experience with Häagen-Dazs feels extraordinary. Creativity is what keeps us innovating and evolving to surprise our customers time and time again.
