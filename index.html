ABOUT US

Häagen-Dazs™ have been making ice cream like no other since 1960. Our founder, Reuben Mattus’ vision was to make the most extraordinary ice cream the world has ever tasted, a spoonful of luxury to escape the everyday. And to get there he only used the highest quality, carefully selected ingredients. As Reuben said, ‘If you’re like everyone else, you’re lost’. So we make an ice cream like no other – the best that best can be.

VALUES

QUALITY

CAt Häagen-Dazs, QUALITY is not just a word—it’s the heart of everything we do. From the moment we source our ingredients to the final scoop in your bowl, we uphold the highest standards to ensure every bite is a moment of indulgence. We use only the finest, all-natural ingredients, including pure cream, fresh milk, and carefully selected flavors like Madagascar vanilla and Belgian chocolate. Each step in our production process is meticulously controlled to preserve the richness, texture, and taste that have defined our brand for decades. When you choose Häagen-Dazs, you’re choosing a product crafted with care and dedication, because we believe that exceptional QUALITY is the foundation of extraordinary taste.

SUSTAINABILITY

At Häagen-Dazs, we recognize our responsibility to care for the planet we all share. That’s why SUSTAINABILITY is at the core of our operations. From sourcing ingredients responsibly to adopting eco-friendly packaging solutions, we are committed to minimizing our environmental impact. Our dairy suppliers adhere to sustainable farming practices that prioritize animal welfare and reduce carbon footprints. Additionally, we work to ensure that every step of our supply chain contributes to a healthier planet, including our efforts to reduce water and energy consumption. SUSTAINABILITY isn’t just about preserving resources—it’s about leaving a legacy of environmental stewardship for generations to come. Together, we can savor our favorite flavors while protecting the world around us.

CREATIVITY

Häagen-Dazs isn’t just about ice cream—it’s about pushing the boundaries of what’s possible in flavor and design. CREATIVITY is at the heart of every product we develop, from innovative flavors like Salted Caramel and Matcha Green Tea to limited-edition collaborations that surprise and delight our customers. We believe that ice cream is a canvas for artistic expression, and we strive to bring that artistry to life in every pint and scoop. Beyond flavors, we’re constantly reimagining how we connect with our customers, from unique packaging designs to inspiring advertising campaigns. Our goal is to inspire joy and spark imagination, ensuring that every experience with Häagen-Dazs feels extraordinary. CREATIVITY is what keeps us innovating and evolving to surprise our customers time and time again.
